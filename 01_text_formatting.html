Amazing Facts About Space

Fact 1: The Sun

The Sun is so large that about one million Earths could fit inside it.
It's mostly made of hydrogen (H2) and helium.

Fact 2: Moon's Distance

The Moon is moving away from Earth by about 3.8cm every year.

Fact 3: Time in Space

Astronauts aboard the ISS experience 16 sunrises and sunsets every 24 hours.
